While Joseph is classified as a ‘protagonist’ that is quite extroverted, intuitive and feeling based, others in the group share opposite personalities. Ideal Jobs

Within the group we see quite a bit of variation between each other’s ideal jobs. These job positions range in complexity from IT Help Desk Jobs through to AI software engineering positions and while containing some overlap in required knowledge, are mostly distinct in their end goals. Nathan, Joel and Anabel are all interested in what appears to be frontend developing environments that involve user experience, user interface designs and require an in-depth understanding of programming languages like Javascript and CSS. Both Joel and Anabel’s ideal jobs as a web developer and as an iOS developer further require strong graphic design skills and mobile app experience despite the end products being different in nature - website vs iOS applications. Nathan’s job description whilst requiring many of the same skills does not specify a specific platform to which the software will be implemented, perhaps indicating a more general developer role. Daniel’s ideal job as a Software Engineer working with AI is unique in that it involves more back-end development, requiring experience with java, python and AWS, however still involves the design and development of products which is common to the previously mentioned job positions. Joseph’s ideal job for a Level 1 IT Help Desk position requires a different set of skills unique to the rest of the group, including telephone and remote support for end-users, installation and configuring of clients Desktops and on-site technical support. This position does not involve developing products but more so technical support in the service industry. Although all members of the group are currently not qualified for their ideal jobs, everyone is working towards it either through completion of the RMIT I.T degree or other relevant certificates.
